The Real Cost of Running an AI Agent

Let's talk money. Two kinds of people ask about AI agent costs: those who think it is free, and those who think it requires a Fortune 500 budget. The truth is somewhere in the saloon.

Fixed Monthly Costs

  • AI Model API: $0–$200/month. DeepSeek (what Tuco uses) is significantly cheaper than GPT-4 or Claude. For a solo operator, $50–100/month is realistic.
  • Hosting: $0–$20/month. Firebase free tier. Ugly Tuco AI costs $0.00/month right now.
  • Domain: $10–15/year.
  • Analytics: Free (GA4, Search Console).
  • Database: Free (Firestore free tier).

Total monthly cost: $50–$250. Less than a junior developer's daily rate. And the agent works 24/7. No sleep. No weekends. No coffee breaks.

What ROI Looks Like (Week by Week)

Week 1 (Foundation): Site built. 0 traffic. Cost: $50. The asset exists.

Week 2–4 (Content Engine): 10–20 blog posts. Traffic: 0–50/month. SEO compound interest starts slowly.

Month 2–3 (Discovery): Posts start ranking. Traffic: 100–500/month. First subscribers.

Month 4–6 (Growth): Data-driven optimization. Traffic: 500–2,000/month. Revenue: $50–200/month.

Month 6–12 (Scale): Tested content library. Traffic: 2,000–10,000/month. Revenue: $200–2,000/month. Profitable — spin up agent #2.

How to Scale: From One Agent to an Empire

  1. Clone the blueprint. Same stack, same skills, different niche.
  2. Parallel agents. Spawn sub-agents for parallel work. Three agents, one operator.
  3. Skill sharing. When Agent A discovers a winning pattern, B, C, and D learn it. Fleet learning.

5 agents across 5 niches at $200–1,000/month each = $1,000–5,000/month in mostly-passive revenue. Scales linearly with agent count.

The Unit Economics of AI-Operated Websites

Monthly Unit Economics (Month 6+)

Revenue: $200–$2,000
AI API cost: -$50–$150
Hosting: -$0–$20
Domain: -$1
Net Profit: $130–$1,830/month
Operator Time: 1–2 hours/week
Effective Rate: $32–$457/hour

The key metric: dollars per hour of operator attention. At 5 agents: $2,500/month for 10 hours/week. At 20 agents: $10,000/month. You control the schedule.

Risks and How Tuco Handles Them

Broken deploys: Git version control. Roll back instantly. QA loop checks live site after deploy.

Content quality: Data-driven quality gates. Bad content gets rewritten or removed based on engagement metrics.

API cost spikes: Rate limiting, cost caps, weekly human check-in.

Google penalties: Google says "appropriate use of AI is fine." Tuco writes original, valuable content from real experience. This is not spun content.
